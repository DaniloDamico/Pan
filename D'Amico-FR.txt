1)The system shall provide a customizable** page for each user. 

2)The system shall provide a tag system to find events*.

3)The system shall provide a group chat for users partecipating in an event. 
    3.1) The activist that created the event can moderate the chat.
    3.2) The activist that created the event can appoint users to act as moderators for the group chat.


*event: a functionality provided by the system to encourage users to consume products of their choosing with set specifications in a limited time frame. Only activists and brand managers can create events.
    The creator of the event specifies its duration and the specifications needed to partecipate in it.

**customizable: users can customize their name, their profile picture and the list of their personal goals.
